02/05/2025 Project 1 Update: My first project will aim to create an industry-standard Intelligence Report. To do this, I have selected the Gallipoli Campaign of World War I to focus on for this project. I have outlined the steps that are needed to accomplish this below.

Step 1:

Gather OSINT from news sources, government reports, open-source platforms, and organize data.

Step 2:

Structure report via IPB Method (Intelligence Preparation of the Battlefield) by defining Operational Environment (OE), describing battlefield effects, evaluating threats, and determining Course(s) of Action (COA).

Step 3:

Include assessments, COAs, and gaps in intelligence to provide a clear analysis, outlining various scenarios and outcomes, risks, and responsiveness, and address insufficient intelligence by recommendations to address the gaps.

Step 4:

Final review and editing of report through proofreading, ensuring logically supported analysis via the available data, and possible peer review.
